Privacy Policy Last updated: March 2026 Introduction ColorSolve ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app. Information We Collect We collect the following information: email address (for account creation), photos you upload for palette generation (processed but not stored), and usage data including palette generations and subscription status. How We Use Your Information We use your information to provide AI palette generation, manage your account and subscription, and display your saved palettes. Third Party Services We use the following third-party services: Supabase (authentication and database), Groq API (AI image analysis — images are processed but not retained), and Apple (payments via In-App Purchase). Data Retention Account data is retained until you delete your account. Uploaded photos are not stored — they are sent to our AI provider for analysis only. Your Rights You may delete your account at any time from the Settings page, which removes all your data.
